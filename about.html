Edward’s furniture design and showroom has been in business for more than half a decade. Mr Edward Khatchatourian the founder of Edwards furniture was an Iranian Arminian artist and furniture designer. He learned to make furniture during his teenage years. His creatively, talent and ambition drove him to latter became the owner of Edwards showroom with a standout collection of furniture at his time. During that time frame most houses and lots of places were designed by Edwards furniture. Including the the royal palace (khakh Neavaran)which is now known as a historical museum in Iran, has also been furnished by Edward’s. Edwards furniture definitely earned its spot in the history of designing and manufacturing high quality furniture in Iran. Edwards furniture is not only well known for it’s design, quality, Variety and comfort, but also paying attention to the smallest detail made its art work to be known as one the best. Some antique shops and dealers in Iran are selling Edward’s old furniture as antique and unique peace’s. Mr Ariuna Edward Khatchatourian the son of mr Edward started working closely with his father after graduating as an architect from France. He came back to Iran to keep the family business. After his father’s death with his vision and experience throughout the years he brought Edward’s furniture to a different level, with more verity in design,upgrading quality for serving new modern living.
